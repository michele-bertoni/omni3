◆ wheels

Wheel* Omni3::wheels[WHEELS_NUM] {}
private

Wheels array elements are ordered as follows: looking the robot from the top, place an imaginary clock dial where the positive forward direction of the robot is at 12 o'clock; proceeding clockwise:

  • wheels[W_RIGHT] Wheel will be at 2 o'clock
  • wheels[W_BACK] Wheel will be at 6 o'clock
  • wheels[W_LEFT] Wheel will be at 10 o'clock
